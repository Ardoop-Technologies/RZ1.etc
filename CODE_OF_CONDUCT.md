# CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md – Community Standards

**Ethical AI MY – Code of Conduct for Participants**

---

## Our Commitment

Ethical AI MY is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for all participants. We are dedicated to creating a community where diverse perspectives are valued, constructive dialogue is encouraged, and all participants feel safe and respected.

---

## Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:

### Respect and Inclusion
- Treat all participants with respect and dignity
- Welcome diverse perspectives and backgrounds
- Listen actively and engage in good faith
- Recognize and value different experiences and viewpoints

### Constructive Engagement
- Participate in discussions with intent to learn and improve
- Provide feedback that is specific, actionable, and respectful
- Focus on ideas and approaches rather than personal attributes
- Assume good intent while addressing harmful impact

### Transparency and Honesty
- Communicate openly about positions, conflicts, and concerns
- Acknowledge mistakes and work toward resolution
- Avoid deception or misrepresentation
- Support documentation and transparency

### Community Care
- Support fellow community members
- Speak up against harmful behavior
- Report concerns through appropriate channels
- Actively work to maintain a welcoming environment

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are unacceptable and will not be tolerated:

### Harassment and Discrimination
- Harassment based on protected characteristics (race, gender, age, religion, etc.)
- Discrimination or exclusion
- Unwelcome sexual or romantic advances
- Sustained disruptive behavior

### Disrespect and Aggression
- Insulting or demeaning language
- Threats or intimidation
- Aggressive or hostile tone
- Personal attacks

### Bad Faith Engagement
- Deliberate misinformation or dishonesty
- Intentional derailment of discussions
- Spam or off-topic promotion
- Manipulation or coercion

### Privacy and Confidentiality Violations
- Sharing private information without consent
- Recording conversations without permission
- Violation of confidentiality agreements
- Doxxing or sharing personal details

---

## Reporting Mechanisms

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

### Reporting Options
1. **Direct communication** – Address concerns directly with the person if safe and comfortable
2. **Issue reporting** – Use GitHub issues for public concerns
3. **Private reporting** – Email maintainers for sensitive issues
4. **Third-party reporting** – Seek support from trusted community members

### What to Include in Reports
- Description of incident or behavior
- Context and impact
- Parties involved (if comfortable sharing)
- Any supporting evidence
- Preferred outcomes or resolution

### Confidentiality
Reports are treated with confidentiality to the extent possible. Reporters will not be retaliated against for good-faith reporting.

---

## Conflict Resolution

### Resolution Process

1. **Initial Response** – Acknowledge receipt and concern
2. **Investigation** – Gather information from involved parties
3. **Discussion** – Engage parties in resolution discussion
4. **Determination** – Apply code of conduct standards
5. **Action** – Implement appropriate consequences
6. **Follow-up** – Verify compliance and ongoing appropriateness

### Possible Consequences

- Verbal warning and discussion
- Written warning
- Temporary suspension from community
- Permanent removal from community
- Escalation to external authorities if necessary

---

## Diversity and Inclusion

We actively:

- Welcome participants from diverse backgrounds
- Encourage underrepresented voices
- Work to address bias in decision-making
- Support accessibility and inclusion
- Recognize and value different perspectives
- Address systemic barriers to participation

---

## Commitment to Improvement

This code of conduct will be:

- Reviewed regularly for effectiveness
- Updated based on community feedback
- Applied consistently and fairly
- Communicated clearly to all participants
- Continuously improved through learning

---

## Questions and Suggestions

If you have questions about this code of conduct or suggestions for improvement, please:

- Open an issue for discussion
- Contact maintainers directly
- Engage in community forums
- Provide feedback respectfully
