## MAIN BODY TEXT

### What is Ethical AI MY?

Ethical AI MY is a comprehensive, publicly available reference for the responsible and ethical development of artificial intelligence systems in Malaysia. The framework provides:

1. **Ethical Framework** – Five core principles (Fairness, Transparency, Accountability, Privacy, Benefit)
2. **Governance Model** – Non-centralized, autonomous stakeholder decision-making
3. **Security Standards** – Technical and operational security requirements
4. **Community Guidelines** – Code of conduct and collaborative principles
5. **Attribution Framework** – Recognition and transparency mechanisms

### Why This Matters

- **Global Significance:** Addresses AI ethics challenges relevant worldwide
- **Malaysian Context:** Tailored to Malaysia's unique AI development environment
- **Stakeholder Focused:** Serves organizations, developers, researchers, communities
- **Open & Accessible:** Available under CC BY 4.0 for broad adoption
- **Non-Mandatory:** Guidance framework, not regulatory mandate
- **Auditable:** Complete transparency in all frameworks and decisions

### Core Innovation: Non-Centralized Governance

Unlike traditional top-down governance models, Ethical AI MY enables:

- **Autonomous Decision-Making:** Stakeholders make independent choices within ethical principles
- **Alignment Through Values:** Shared ethical principles maintain cohesion
- **Transparent Accountability:** All decisions documented and available for review
- **Collaborative Problem-Solving:** Stakeholders work together on shared challenges
- **Community Evolution:** Framework improves based on collective learning

### Technical Highlights

**Complete Repository Contents:**
- 6 core policy documents (ETHICS, GOVERNANCE, SECURITY, CODE_OF_CONDUCT, ATTRIBUTION, FINAL_INTENT)
- Comprehensive README with governance diagrams

**Accessibility Features:**
