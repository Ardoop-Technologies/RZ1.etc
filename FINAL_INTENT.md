# FINAL_INTENT.md – Statement of Purpose and Intent

**Ethical AI MY – Long-Term Vision and Objectives**

---

## Statement of Intent

Ethical AI MY is established as an open, auditable, non-mandatory reference for the responsible and ethical development of artificial intelligence systems in Malaysia. This initiative is undertaken with the following intentions and commitments:

---

## Primary Objectives

### 1. Establish Ethical Foundation

To create and maintain a comprehensive, evidence-based framework of ethical principles applicable to AI development and deployment in the Malaysian context.

**Intended Outcomes:**
- Clear articulation of ethical standards
- Practical guidance for implementation
- Accessibility to all stakeholders
- Continuous evolution based on learning

### 2. Enable Non-Centralized Governance

To establish governance structures that empower stakeholders to make autonomous decisions while maintaining alignment through shared ethical principles.

**Intended Outcomes:**
- Distributed decision-making authority
- Transparent accountability mechanisms
- Community engagement and participation
- Collaborative problem-solving

### 3. Promote Stakeholder Alignment

To facilitate alignment among diverse stakeholders—organizations, developers, researchers, policymakers, and communities—on ethical principles and practices.

**Intended Outcomes:**
- Shared understanding of ethical standards
- Collaborative problem-solving
- Reduced conflicts through alignment
- Strengthened community bonds

### 4. Support Responsible AI Development

To provide practical resources and guidance enabling organizations and developers to implement ethical principles in their AI systems.

**Intended Outcomes:**
- Accessible implementation guidance
- Technical security standards
- Community support and learning
- Recognition and accountability

---

## Core Commitments

### Commitment to Openness

- All materials are publicly available
- No proprietary or closed components
- Transparent decision-making processes
- Open to community participation
- Encouragement of derivative works

### Commitment to Auditability

- Complete documentation of frameworks
- Transparent governance mechanisms
- Accessible review processes
- Verifiable implementation standards
- Accountability for decisions

### Commitment to Non-Mandate

- Clear statement that this is guidance, not regulation
- No enforcement authority or power
- Recognition of contextual variation
- Support for adaptation and customization
- Respect for organizational autonomy

### Commitment to Continuous Evolution

- Regular review of frameworks
- Incorporation of stakeholder feedback
- Adaptation to changing context
- Integration of new research
- Transparent versioning and communication

## Values and Principles Guiding Implementation

### Inclusivity
Ensure all stakeholder voices are heard and represented in governance and decision-making.

### Transparency
Maintain openness in all processes, decisions, and communications.

### Respect
Recognize and value diverse perspectives and expertise.

### Learning
Commit to continuous learning and improvement based on experience.

### Accountability
Take responsibility for outcomes and engage in honest assessment.

### Humility
Recognize limitations and areas for improvement.

---

## What This Is NOT

### Not a Mandate
This reference does not impose requirements. Organizations are not obligated to adopt these principles.

### Not a Regulation
Ethical AI MY is not a legal or regulatory instrument. It complements but does not replace legal and regulatory frameworks.

### Not Centrally Controlled
There is no central authority dictating implementation. Stakeholders make independent decisions.

### Not Final or Complete
This is a living document subject to evolution as understanding improves.

### Not a Substitute for Domain Expertise
Organizations must apply principles with contextual understanding specific to their domain.

## Success Criteria

Ethical AI MY will be considered successful if:

1. **Adoption** – Broadly adopted by stakeholders as reference material
2. **Impact** – Demonstrated positive impact on AI development practices
3. **Evolution** – Continuously improved through community engagement
4. **Accessibility** – Easily understood and implemented by diverse audiences
5. **Accountability** – Clear mechanisms for oversight and accountability
6. **Community** – Strong, engaged, and inclusive community of practitioners
7. **Integration** – Integration into policies, practices, and education
